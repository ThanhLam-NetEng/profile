About Me

I am a final-year student at the University of Information Technology (UIT – VNU-HCM), majoring in Computer Networks & Data Communications (GPA: 7.72/10). My focus is on network security, system infrastructure, and network automation.

Outside the classroom, I have been involved in real internship environments — configuring Cisco Catalyst switches, deploying SD-WAN solutions on Peplink devices, operating fiber infrastructure at a data center, and implementing threat mitigation systems. These experiences have sharpened my ability to move quickly between theoretical knowledge and practical, enterprise-grade deployment.

My current thesis focuses on a High Availability firewall architecture integrated with a SIEM system for automated log analysis, using simulated attack scenarios to evaluate system resilience. It also applies AI techniques at the feature engineering level to extract insights from log data. Over the next 2–3 years, I aim to further develop my expertise in Network Automation and Cloud Networking.
